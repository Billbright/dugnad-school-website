Our Story

More than just a school, we are a community.

History & Heritage

Founded to serve the local community, Dugnad School has grown from a humble classroom to a center of academic excellence. "Dugnad" represents the spirit of communal work and cooperation.

We provide a safe, nurturing environment where traditional African values meet modern educational standards.

Our Mission

To empower Ugandan youth through holistic education, character building, and practical skills for a self-reliant future.
